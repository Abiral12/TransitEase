    • Planning Your Adventure from Kathmandu to Gulmi, Tamghas, and Simaltari.

    Published on December 24, 2012

    If you are planning to travel from Kathmandu to Gulmi, Tamghas, and Simaltari, here is some important information to help you plan your journey.

    Journey Duration and Route
  • Kathmandu to Gulmi:

    • The journey from Kathmandu to Gulmi typically takes around 8 to 10 hours. Buses usually depart between 6:00 AM and 7:00 AM and reach Gulmi between 4:00 PM and 5:00 PM.

  • Gulmi to Tamghas:

    • The distance from Gulmi to Tamghas is approximately 45 kilometers, and the travel time is around 2 to 3 hours. Buses typically depart between 7:00 AM and 8:00 AM.

  • Tamghas to Simaltar:

    • The distance from Tamghas to Simaltari is about 20 kilometers, and the travel time is around 1 hour. Buses usually depart between 8:00 AM and 9:00 AM.
